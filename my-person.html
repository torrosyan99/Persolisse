External sides of personality

  • Visionary and strategy: Has the ability to see a holistic picture (vision), to think strategically and predict results.
  • Transformation: Knows how to transform negative situations into positive ones, to change people's minds, to find non-trivial ways out of dead ends.
  • Influence and Effectiveness: Uses their personal power ("might") to create change by acting consciously.
  • Charisma and wisdom: The Magician is often perceived as an expert, a mysterious and attractive personality. He has intuition and the ability to "read" people.
  • Integrity: Seeks to integrate the spiritual and the material, the inner and the outer.

The shadow sides of personality

  • Manipulation: Like a "skilled puppeteer," the Magician in the shadows pulls the right strings, using people and their weaknesses to their advantage.
  • Evil Sorcerer/Manipulator: Turns good into bad, acts behind the scenes, intrigues, and destroys what others have created.
  • Cynicism and detachment: You may become cold, detached, and arrogant, looking down on people.
  • Obsession with power: Using knowledge to control others rather than to develop them.
  • Enjoying the failures of others: A Shadow Mage can feed off of other people's failures to make themselves look more successful.
